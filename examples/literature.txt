## LITERATURE QUIZ ##

1) Who is the author of Things Fall Apart?
a) W. B. Yeats
b) Chinua Achebe
c) Arundhati Roy
Ans: b

2) Which of these novels was written by Emily Bronte?
a) The Tenant of Wildfell Hall
b) Wuthering Heights
c) Jane Eyre
d) Great Expectations
Ans: b

3) Which of these works was published earlier?
a) Pamela, by Samuel Richardson
b) Tristram Shandy, by Laurence Sterne
Ans: a
